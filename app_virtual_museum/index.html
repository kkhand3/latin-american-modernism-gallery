The Latin American Modernism Museum

A virtual gallery exploring Latin American Modernism and its different movements through the ages

By Kaashya Khandelwal

Welcome to the Latin American Modernism Museum. Here we will explore themes of modernism, resistance and transformation across Latin America. We have a special installation titled "Transnationalism and Decolonialism in Latin America," aimed at exploring these topics in depth. To navigate through the museum, click on the arrows to go to different parts of the museum.
